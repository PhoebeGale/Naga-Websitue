Naga Atong Garbo!

The City of Naga, located in the province of Cebu, Philippines, is a vibrant city known for its rich cultural heritage and economic significance. Strategically situated along the southeastern coast of Cebu Island, it serves as a hub for various industries, including manufacturing and energy production.

TOURISM COMITTEE

As of December 2024, the current mayor is Valdemar M. Chiong. His leadership focuses on sustainable development and enhancing the quality of life for Naga's residents. The legislative body compromises several councilors, each chairing specific committees to address various aspects of governance.

Hon. Scott Juvenal Chiong leads the Committee on Tourism Development. His leadership drives initiatives to highlight the city's cultural heritage, promote its natural attractions, and boost local tourism by organizing events and developing strategic partnerships. The committee formulates policies and programs aimed at promoting Naga's Tourism sector.

The city's administration is dedicated to promoting Naga as a prime tourist destination, capitalizing on its historical landmarks, natural attractions, and cultural festivals to attract both local and international visitors. Naga City is not just a place of progress but also a community that values its heritage, natural beauty, and the potential of its people. With its leaders' commitment to sustainable growth and cultural preservation, the city remains a beacon of pride and opportunity in Cebu.
