Explore Museo Naga

Discover Naga City's Rich Heritage

Museo Naga is a vibrant cultural hub in Naga City, Cebu, dedicated to preserving and showcasing the city's rich history, heritage, and natural environment. The museum features diverse galleries, including the Natural History Gallery, Naga Heritage and Contemporary Gallery, and the Leader's Gallery. Visitors can explore interactive displays, multimedia presentations, and a variety of artifacts that tell the story of Naga's past and present.

"Museo Naga: Where history and culture come alive."

Explore Pasilong Sa Naga

Experience Tranquility and Beauty in Naga City's Premier Park

Pasilong sa Naga, located in the heart of Naga City, Cebu, is a serene public park designed by renowned Cebuano designer Kenneth Cobonpue. This beautifully landscaped park features scenic gardens, panoramic views, and various relaxation areas, making it an ideal spot for unwinding and reconnecting with nature. The park's unique architectural elements and tranquil ambiance offer visitors a peaceful escape from the city's hustle and bustle.

"A serene escape crafted for relaxation."

Blu-Pool Waterpark Resort

Dive into Fun and Adventure at Naga City's Premier Waterpark

Blu-Pool Waterpark, located within Guanzon Beach Resort in Naga City, Cebu, is the first water park in southern Cebu. This family-friendly destination offers a variety of amenities, including affordable entrance fees, scenic pool areas, and comfortable cottages. It's an ideal spot for a fun-filled day of swimming and relaxation, with beautiful views of the surrounding area.

"Dive into fun and adventure at Blu-Pool Waterpark."
