Explore Naga Boardwalk

Where tranquility meets the vibrant charm of the sea.

The Naga Boardwalk offers a picturesque escape by the sea, perfect for leisurely strolls, breathtaking sunsets, and vibrant activities. Enjoy the peaceful ambiance during the day and the lively charm of illuminated paths at night. Whether you're looking for relaxation or fun, the boardwalk has something for everyone!

"Feel the sea breeze, hear the calming waves, and let the Naga Boardwalk be your sanctuary."
